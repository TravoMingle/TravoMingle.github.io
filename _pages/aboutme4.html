Hello! I'm Ghea Melinda, a full-time mom from the vibrant city of Bandung, Indonesia, and a blogger with a deep love for writing and exploring. My days are often filled with the beautiful chaos of raising a family, but whenever I get a quiet moment, I turn to my blog and my trusty keyboard. Writing has always been my way of finding peace amidst the busyness of life, a little escape where I can be just me.

My motto? "Writing opens windows to the depths of the soul, revealing layers of truth and emotion that often go unnoticed in everyday life." For me, writing is not just about telling stories—it's about peeling back the layers of life and finding the beauty in those small, often overlooked moments. It helps me express things I sometimes can’t say out loud, and it connects me with others who might feel the same way.

At ninggra.com, I share my thoughts, travels, and experiences as both a mom and a dreamer. I believe that adventure doesn’t stop just because we’re raising a family. There are so many wonderful things to explore, both near and far, and I love writing about everything—from exciting trips to simple family outings in the park. I want to inspire other moms, and anyone reading, to find the joy in every little adventure life offers.

Life as a stay-at-home mom is an adventure all on its own, but my passion for writing and travel keeps me grounded and inspired. My hope is that through this blog, you’ll find encouragement to cherish your own journey, no matter where it takes you.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to share your stories or just say hi! You can email me at gheamelinda26@gmail.com. I'd love to hear from you!

Thanks so much for visiting ninggra.com, and I hope my stories bring a little light and inspiration to your day.

Warmly,
Ghea Melinda
